Search Engine Optimization

We can help your business get found.

S E O

Building an SEO-friendly site requires careful planning and a structured approach to representing your business and the services you provide.Technology is constantly changing and Your Web Design continues to keep current with SEO those changes.

We create a truly mobile-friendly design and maximize results from mobile search, we think of the needs and wants of mobile users. What a user will do on a phone is often far different from what they will do on a computer. And even if your conversions tend to be on a desktop, that crucial first touch may well be on mobile. From an SEO perspective, it is worth noting that mobile-friendliness is a confirmed ranking factor for mobile search.

We make sure the context of your page is clear. Our navigation, URLs, page titles, headers and so on should all help here. Yet we want to write naturally, using synonyms and natural language.

We recommend any new website or domain to utilize SSL encryption. It may also be more important for websites that transmit data frequently, such as e-commerce sites where users must login and input personal or credit card information to complete a transaction.

Google also pays attention to how often you update the content of your site and how significant those updates are. For example, if you rewrite the entirety of your homepage with information about your latest products, that registers as more significant than only changing a few words around every few years. It takes extra work to consistently keep your site updated, but it will help you not only earn more authority, but keep your users up to speed as well.

We could continue to offer more examples but, that would require writing a book on the subject. Don't worry we read that book and understand how to get your site found.
